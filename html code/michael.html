2017 Spotify Weekday and Monthly Trends

Insights

Based on this figure from the Verge, half of Spotify's user base have consistently been comprised of non-premium users, whose streaming service is subject to ads. As such, targeted ads are critical to Spotify's non-premium service revenue model. By better understanding daily user activity, Spotify can tailor its advertisements to a user’s listening trends. In addition, Spotify can alter how they price discriminate to advertisers based on the day of the week or month of year. For example, Spotify can increase the price for ad slots on more popular days, and offer deals or discounts on less popular days. Further, Spotify can launch ad campaigns to increase and incentivize usership on historically less popular days.

Moreover, Spotify can adjust its daily mix algorithms and tailor them to a user’s music preferences as it changes throughout the week and year. This adjustment will therefore have the potential to improve the UX for Spotify’s users.

Recommendations

Jazz-related ad slot prices should be increased on Sundays and in December. New Wave-related ad slot prices should be increased on Mondays and in December. Country-related ad slot prices should be increased on Mondays and in September. Hip-hop-related ad slot prices should be increased on Fridays and in October. Rock-related ad slot prices should be increased on Fridays and in July. Pop-related ad slot prices should be increased on Fridays and in March. Electronic-related ad slot prices should be increased on Fridays and in January.
